About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Connecticut, focusing on aligning artificial intelligence with humanity's many goals and moral views. My research interests span AI safety, ethics, and governance. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Shiri Dori-Hacohen in the Reducing Information Ecosystem Threats (RIET) Lab, and I am the founder and president of the student group Beneficial and Ethical AI at UConn (BEACON).
